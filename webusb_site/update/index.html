WebUSB updater for CZ20's STM32

Important: this tool should only be used if you know what you're doing, or were asked to run it. It should not be able to brick your badge, but don't risk it without good reason.

  1. Press and hold the middle 2 buttons of the 2nd row from the top on your badge.
  2. Unplug and replug the USB cable into your badge (still pressing the buttons).
  3. Your badge now starts in DFU mode, and will show a popup in Chrome that makes you go to this site.
  4. Release the buttons, click "connect" above, and select "HackZone DFU".
  5. Click "Program" and wait a couple dozen seconds for a popup that tells you if it worked.
  6. Unplug and plug in the USB cable again. Done!
